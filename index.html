The Glass & Crystal Museum

This digital archive presents a private collection of author's objects from the Museum of the Pervomaisky Glass Factory. The collection includes unique examples of handmade artistic glass, traditional blown glass (gutta glass), complete art ensembles, crystal vases and decorative tableware created by the factory's master craftsmen.

Each piece represents the heritage of Russian glassmaking, combining craftsmanship, artistic vision and the beauty of hand-shaped crystal and glass.
